The hair cycle is influenced by both internal biology and external factors like stress and UV exposure, especially in spring & summer. Chronic sun exposure can speed up thinning, often causing scalp damage before visible hair loss. We've noticed that hair loss tends to rise among patients likely due to seasonal shifts, androgenic alopecia, and stress-related aging of the scalp.
